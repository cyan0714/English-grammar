### 整十的基数词
- 20 - twenty
- 30 - thirty  
- 40 - forty
- 50 - fifty
- 60 - sixty
- 70 - seventy
- 80 - eighty
- 90 - ninety

### 大数的基数词
- 100 - one hundred
- 1,000 - one thousand
- 10,000 - ten thousand
- 100,000 - one hundred thousand
- 1,000,000 - one million
- 1,000,000,000 - one billion

### 基数词的用法规则

1. **21-99的复合数词用连字符**：
   - 21 - twenty-one
   - 35 - thirty-five
   - 89 - eighty-nine

2. **三位数的读法**：
   - 101 - one hundred and one
   - 205 - two hundred and five
   - 999 - nine hundred and ninety-nine

3. **基数词的实用例句**：
   - I have **two** books. (我有两本书)
   - There are **twenty-three** students in our class. (我们班有23个学生)
   - **Eight** is a lucky number. (八是一个幸运数字)
   - She bought **five hundred** apples. (她买了500个苹果)

## 2. 序数词 (Ordinal Numbers)

### 1-20的序数词
| 基数词 | 序数词 | 缩写 |
|--------|--------|------|
| one | first | 1st |
| two | second | 2nd |
| three | third | 3rd |
| four | fourth | 4th |
| five | fifth | 5th |
| six | sixth | 6th |
| seven | seventh | 7th |
| eight | eighth | 8th |
| nine | ninth | 9th |
| ten | tenth | 10th |
| eleven | eleventh | 11th |
| twelve | twelfth | 12th |
| thirteen | thirteenth | 13th |
| twenty | twentieth | 20th |

### 序数词的构成规则

1. **一般规则**：基数词 + th
   - four → fourth
   - six → sixth
   - seven → seventh

2. **特殊变化**：
   - one → first
   - two → second  
   - three → third
   - five → fifth (去e加th)
   - eight → eighth (去t加th)
   - nine → ninth (去e加th)
   - twelve → twelfth (ve变f加th)

3. **整十数的序数词**：
   - twenty → twentieth (y变ie加th)
   - thirty → thirtieth
   - forty → fortieth

### 序数词的用法

1. **序数词作主语一般加 the**：
   - **The first** is important. (第一个很重要)
   - **The third** student answered correctly. (第三个学生回答正确)

2. **表示日期**：
   - March **21st** (3月21日)
   - the **fifteenth** of June (6月15日)

3. **表示楼层**：
   - I live on the **fifth** floor. (我住在五楼)
   - His office is on the **twentieth** floor. (他的办公室在二十楼)

4. **表示顺序**：
   - This is my **first** time in Beijing. (这是我第一次来北京)
   - She won **second** place in the competition. (她在比赛中获得第二名)

## 3. 分数 (Fractions)

### 分数的读法
- 1/2 - one half 或 a half
- 1/3 - one third 或 a third
- 1/4 - one quarter 或 a quarter
- 2/3 - two thirds
- 3/4 - three quarters
- 5/8 - five eighths

### 分数的用法规则
1. **分子用基数词，分母用序数词**
2. **分子大于1时，分母用复数**
3. **例句**：
   - **One third** of the students are from China. (三分之一的学生来自中国)
   - I spent **two thirds** of my salary. (我花了我工资的三分之二)

## 4. 小数 (Decimals)

### 小数的读法
- 0.5 - zero point five 或 nought point five
- 1.25 - one point two five
- 3.14 - three point one four
- 0.01 - zero point zero one

### 小数的用法例句
- The temperature is **twenty-three point five** degrees. (温度是23.5度)
- My height is **one point seven five** meters. (我的身高是1.75米)

## 5. 百分数 (Percentages)

### 百分数的读法和写法
- 50% - fifty percent
- 25% - twenty-five percent  
- 100% - one hundred percent
- 0.5% - zero point five percent

### 百分数的用法例句
- **Ninety percent** of the work is finished. (90%的工作已经完成)
- The price increased by **fifteen percent**. (价格上涨了15%)

## 6. 倍数 (Multiples)

### 倍数的表达方式
1. **A is ... times as + 形容词 + as B**：
   - This room is **three times as large as** that one. (这个房间是那个房间的三倍大)

2. **A is ... times + 比较级 + than B**：
   - This building is **twice taller than** that one. (这栋楼比那栋楼高两倍)

3. **A is ... times the size/length/width of B**：
   - This table is **four times the size of** that one. (这张桌子是那张桌子的四倍大)

## 7. 数词的常见错误

### 错误示例及纠正：
1. ❌ I have twenty-one years old.  
   ✅ **I am twenty-one years old.** (年龄用be动词)

2. ❌ This is my one time to visit Beijing.  
   ✅ **This is my first time to visit Beijing.** (表示"第一次"用序数词)

3. ❌ He lives on five floor.  
   ✅ **He lives on the fifth floor.** (楼层用序数词，且加the)

4. ❌ Two third of students passed the exam.  
   ✅ **Two thirds of the students passed the exam.** (分子大于1，分母用复数)
